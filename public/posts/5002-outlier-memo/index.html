5002 Outlier Memo

Comparison

Statistical Model Disadv: Assume that the data follows a particular distribution. Distance-based Model Adv: No assume distribution. But not density wise. Density-Based Model Adv: can find local outliers

Concept

$\epsilon$ is the distance between p and the k-th nearest neighbor. local reachability density lrd_k(p) is: $\frac 1 \epsilon$ local outlier factor(LOF) is $(\sum_{o \in N_k(p)} \frac{lrd_k(o)}{lrd_k(p)})/{k}$ N_k(p) is the $\epsilon$-neighborhood of p (excluding p).
